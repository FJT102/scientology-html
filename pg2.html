Scientology is a set of beliefs invented by the American author L. Ron Hubbard. origionally his idea was a form of talk therapy called Dianetics. He later expanded and changed those ideas as a religion, which he named Scientology. In 1953 he founded the Church of Scientology, which by one 2014 estimate had measured it at around 30,000 members.

Scientology has been described as a religion, a cult, a business, and a scam. Scientology is classified differently around the world, with some countries granting it religious status, while others treat it as a non-religious belief system.
